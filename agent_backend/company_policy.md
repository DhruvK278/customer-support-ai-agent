# Keyboard E-Commerce Company Policies

## Refunds and Returns
- Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of delivery if the product is unopened.
- If the product is defective upon arrival (major functionality problem or safety concern), a full refund or free replacement is guaranteed.
- Minor cosmetic defects (scratches, small dents) do not qualify for a full refund. Offer a Discount (up to 20%) or Store Credit.

## Replacements
- Replacements are only issued for hardware failures, missing components, or receiving the Incorrect Item.
- Replacements take 3-5 business days to process.

## Service Issues & Apologies
- For delayed shipments (Service Issues) taking longer than 10 days, issue a formal Apology and a 10% Discount on the next order.
- Do not offer Compensation exceeding $50 for any service-related delays.
